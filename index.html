TenanterApp, the next steps...

Sept 2021

Fist Beta Test

First TenanterApp beta release covering basic features, such as: multiple property creation, payment logging & notifications, financial stats and tenant info page. 

Dec 2021

TenanterApp for tenants

Even if we start small, we have something really big in mind: a platform for landlords and tenants that benefits both parties equally. After feedback collection we will improve and extend existing features and make the app also accessible for tenants. We want to make communication between tenants and landlords as easy as possible.

Feb 2022

In-app Payments and bank account synchronization...

Yes, you heard it right, we will introduce in-app payments for tenants so both parties can benefit from secure and reliable payment processing. Regardless of whether payments are used or not, landlords should also be able to sync their bank account within the app in order to have a better overview of incoming payments.

FAQ

Can I use the TeanterApp for free?

TenanterApp Beta is free for use without any limitations. We will introduce Pro plans on official release.

How my data is used? Will you sell my data to third party?

All user data is saved securely on our servers. We collect anonymized user data to improve user experience. We will never sell or hand over any user data to third party for commercial purpose.

Do I need to register in order to use the app?

You can test the app with limited features without registration. You can create an account if you want to use the app on regular basis.

How can I submit feedback?

Just get in touch with us within app or drop us a message to support@tenanterapp.com
